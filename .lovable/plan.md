

# The Quill – Restaurant Website

## Overview
A modern, elegant restaurant website for The Quill – Restaurant in Korinda, featuring warm colors (deep brown, gold, cream, dark green), luxury typography, and smooth animations. Mobile-first, responsive design.

---

## Pages & Features

### 1. Homepage
- **Hero Section** — Full-width background image with tagline "Experience Flavor Without Limits – Open 24 Hours" and CTA buttons ("View Menu" / "Order Now")
- **Quick Highlights** — Rating (3.9⭐ / 143 reviews), price range (Ksh 500–1,000), 24-hour badge
- **Services Strip** — Dine-in, Takeaway, Delivery icons
- **Featured Dishes** — 3-4 popular menu items as preview cards
- **Food Gallery** — Grid of appetizing food photos
- **Newsletter Subscription** — Email signup section

### 2. Menu Page
- Categorized tabs: Starters, Main Dishes, Drinks, Specials
- Food cards with image, name, description, price, and "Popular" badges
- Filter/category navigation

### 3. About Us
- Restaurant story and values
- Emphasis on 24-hour service, affordability, and customer satisfaction
- Visual imagery alongside text

### 4. Reviews Section
- Overall rating display (3.9/5 with star visuals)
- Sample customer review cards
- Review submission form (name, rating, comment)

### 5. Contact & Location
- Click-to-call phone button (0113 857846)
- Embedded Google Maps for C4XP+MH Korinda, B1
- Contact form (name, email, message)
- Operating hours display (Open 24 hours)

### 6. Order Online
- Three tabs: Dine-in Reservation, Takeaway Order, Delivery Request
- Forms with item selection, date/time, contact details
- Confirmation message on submission

---

## Global Elements
- **Sticky Navigation Bar** — Logo, page links, "Order Now" button
- **WhatsApp Chat Button** — Floating button linking to WhatsApp
- **Footer** — Contact info, social media links, quick links, share/save options
- **Smooth scroll animations** throughout
- **Mobile-first responsive design** with hamburger menu

---

## Design Direction
- **Colors**: Deep brown, gold, cream backgrounds, dark green accents
- **Typography**: Elegant serif headings (Playfair Display style), clean sans-serif body text
- **Feel**: Warm, luxurious, inviting

## Technical Notes
- No backend needed — all forms will show confirmation toasts (can be connected to a backend later)
- Static menu data stored in code
- Sample review data with local-only submission
- SEO meta tags for restaurant keywords
